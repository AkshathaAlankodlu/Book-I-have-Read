Mahasweta

Author: Sudha Murthy

Genre: Fiction, Romance

Rating: 4

Blurb: Dr.Anand and Anupama who are in their early ages of their career fall in love eventually marrying eachother. It is only after fews days of their marriage that Anand's mom finds out Anupama has Leukoderma - she humiliates her and abandons her while Anand was out to persue his masters.The book takes us through the hardships in Anupama's life and the harsh reality of Indian society.

It was the first ever book I tired in romance genre when I was in class 9. Being my first read i was excited throughout the book carrying it to my music class and reading it in the fews minutes i got before the class began.It would have got me into the the habit of reading if my mom woudn't have insisted me to stop reading books so that it dosen't affect my studies.

Of Course I Love You

Author: Durjoy Datta

Genre: Fiction, Romance

Rating: 3

Blurb: Deb is a college student who jumps from one casual relationship to another. Everything changes when he falls for Avantika and until she leaves him, forcing him to face heartbreak and figure out his life himself.

Review:After a long gap to reading Books i read this book with a smile on my face. It look me months to finally finish it.Though it marked a starting point to my long lost hobby of reading books.

I Too Had a Love Story

Author: Ravinder Singh

Genre: Based on True Story, Romance

Rating: 4.2

Blurb: Ravinder an IT professional meets Kushi on a matrimonial website ,instantly falls in love and decides to marry her. But life takes unexpected turns leaving him devastrated.

Review: A well written book with real emotions which kept me flipping through pages till the end.

Life Is What We Make It

Author: Preeti Shenoy

Genre: Fiction, Romance

Rating: 4

Blurb: Ankita is a bright and happy college girl, but her life suddenly changes when she begins struggling with her mental health. She ends up in a hospital and must work hard to rebuild her life. This story shows how tough times can be faced with strength and hope.

Review: The story becomes traumatic at times .I got so involved into the book I felt as if i were the lead charecter. The story shows on how you can rebuild a happy, meaningful life with courage and determination even after facing pain in the past.
